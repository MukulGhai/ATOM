# AtomQuest: Roles and Features Guide

The AtomQuest platform uses strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). There are three distinct login methods/roles: **Employee**, **Manager**, and **Admin**. Below is a detailed breakdown of what each role can access and exactly what actions they can perform.

---

## 1. Employee
**Demo Login**: `employee1@atomquest.com`
**Password**: `emp123`

The Employee role focuses on personal performance management, goal submission, and quarterly tracking.

### Accessible Pages & Capabilities:
* **Dashboard (`/dashboard`)**:
  - View personal statistics (total goals, pending approvals, average progress).
  - View a quick summary of active goals and recent alerts.
* **Goals (`/goals`)**:
  - Create new personal goals (Drafts).
  - Define weightage (min 10%), target dates, and metrics.
  - Submit goal sets to their Manager for approval (Total weightage must exactly equal 100%).
  - Share goals with peers via a relational link.
* **Check-ins (`/checkins`)**:
  - Log quarterly check-ins for active goals.
  - Update numerical progress and leave descriptive status notes (e.g., "On Track", "At Risk").
* **Escalations (`/escalations`)**:
  - View system-generated warnings triggered by their actions (e.g., missed deadlines, stagnant goals).
  - Click **"Acknowledge"** on open escalations to notify management they are actively working on resolving the issue.
* **Analytics (`/analytics`)**:
  - View personal performance charts and visual breakdowns of goal completion.
* **Notifications (`/notifications`)**:
  - Receive real-time Socket.IO alerts for manager approvals, rejections, or new escalations.

---

## 2. Manager
**Demo Login**: `manager@atomquest.com`
**Password**: `manager123`

The Manager role includes everything an Employee can do (for their own personal goals), plus advanced permissions to review and guide their direct reports.

### Accessible Pages & Capabilities:
* **Dashboard (`/dashboard`)**:
  - View aggregated team statistics.
  - See a table of "Team Performance" detailing direct reports' progress.
* **Approvals (`/approvals`)** *(Manager/Admin Exclusive)*:
  - Review goal sets submitted by employees.
  - **Approve** goals, changing their status to "Active".
  - **Reject** goals and leave mandatory feedback on why it was rejected.
  - **Inline Edit**: Adjust weightages or metrics directly without needing the employee to resubmit.
* **Check-ins (`/checkins`)**:
  - View a dedicated "Team Check-ins" table to monitor reports' quarterly progress.
* **Escalations (`/escalations`)**:
  - View escalations generated across their entire team.
  - Click **"Resolve"** to close an escalation once the employee has fixed the issue.
  - Click **"Run Check"** to manually trigger the backend escalation engine to scan the team for new violations.
* **All Employee Pages**: Full access to create personal goals, view analytics, etc.

---

## 3. Admin
**Demo Login**: `admin@atomquest.com`
**Password**: `admin123`

The Admin has supreme access over the entire system, managing organizational structure, user access, and viewing global analytics.

### Accessible Pages & Capabilities:
* **Users (`/users`)** *(Admin Exclusive)*:
  - Complete User Management dashboard.
  - **Create Users**: Add new employees to the platform.
  - **Assign Roles & Managers**: Set organizational hierarchy (e.g., assigning an employee to a specific manager).
  - **Deactivate**: Disable user accounts to revoke platform access.
  - **Edit**: Update emails, departments, and reset passwords.
* **Dashboard & Analytics**:
  - View organization-wide statistics (not just team-level).
  - See global performance distributions and company-wide goal completion metrics.
* **Escalations (`/escalations`)**:
  - See every escalation in the entire company.
  - Resolve any escalation system-wide.
* **Full Access**: Admins inherit all Employee and Manager capabilities and pages.
